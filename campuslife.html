Campus Life at UEP

A vibrant space where history, culture, and innovation meet.

Our History

2005 – Founding of UEP

The University of Eastern Pangasinan was founded with the mission to provide quality, accessible education for all learners.

2010 – Expansion of Programs

UEP introduced new academic programs, research initiatives, and student organizations that foster innovation and leadership.

Present – Growth & Community

Today, UEP is a hub for cultural pride, academic excellence, and strong community engagement in Pangasinan and beyond.

Life at UEP

Sports & Wellness

Our intramurals, sports teams, and fitness facilities encourage teamwork and promote health among students.

Culture & Arts

Through music, dance, and cultural festivals, UEP showcases diverse student talents and strengthens cultural pride.

Community Engagement

UEP instills values of leadership and service through outreach programs, volunteerism, and social responsibility.

Welcome to University of Eastern Pangasinan

At UEP, campus life goes beyond classrooms — it is a journey of discovery, growth, and community. Our university is a place where students find opportunities to learn, connect, and thrive. Together, we embrace a culture of excellence, innovation, and service for the future.
